Helia is designed to work with Chromium-based browsers such as Chrome, Opera, and modern Edge. You may experience issues using non-Chromium based browsers. The server takes ~30 minutes to restart - if you can't log in now, try again later.



Once you've logged in, you can resize your browser to adjust the game window, and enter fullscreen using your browser's native facilities (on Chrome, this is usually F11).
