Big Things Have Small Beginnings.

Somewhere far away in space and time, a race of machines battles themselves and each other for control of the stars... Welcome to Project Helia - an ambitious project to create a new, massively multiplayer, online role playing game! Currently, Helia is in an open alpha state and is free to play during open alpha testing.


Open PVP Everyhere - No Exceptions.

Unlike other games, Helia provides no safe areas or starter zones. PVP mechanics do not depend on your location in the universe - its open season. However, any combat you engage in (whether against a player or an NPC) will adjust your standings, and that may not work well for you in the long term.
